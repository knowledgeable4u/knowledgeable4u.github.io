About Knowledgeable4u

Knowledgeable4u is a science education content platform focused on making complex health and biotech research accessible to everyone. We publish daily short-form videos across multiple platforms including TikTok, YouTube, Instagram, and Facebook.

Our content is researched, scripted, and published using automated tools that integrate with official platform APIs — including the TikTok Content Posting API — to ensure consistent, timely delivery of educational content to our audience.

We are committed to responsible use of platform APIs in accordance with each platform's terms of service and developer policies.
