Knowledgeable4u Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Effective date: July 1, 2026  |  Last updated: July 16, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Knowledgeable4u ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects information when you use our content publishing application, including our integration with the TikTok Content Posting API.

1. Information We Collect

When you authorize Knowledgeable4u to access your TikTok account, we may collect and store the following information:

  • TikTok User ID (open_id) — a unique identifier assigned by TikTok to your account
  • Access Token & Refresh Token — OAuth credentials required to publish content on your behalf
  • Token expiry metadata — timestamps used to determine when tokens must be refreshed

We do not collect your TikTok username, password, email address, phone number, profile photo, follower list, direct messages, or any other personal data beyond what is listed above.

2. How We Use Your Information

The data we collect is used exclusively for the following purpose:

  • Posting science education videos to your TikTok account via the TikTok Content Posting API
  • Refreshing OAuth tokens automatically when they expire so uploads continue uninterrupted

We do not use your data for advertising, analytics, profiling, or any other purpose.

3. Data Storage and Security

All collected data (OAuth tokens and user identifiers) is stored locally on your own device in a file named tiktok_token.json. This file is never transmitted to any external server operated by Knowledgeable4u.

We recommend keeping this file private and not sharing it with others, as it grants publish access to your TikTok account.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, trade, or otherwise share your personal information with any third parties, with the exception of TikTok's own API infrastructure which is required to publish videos on your behalf.

Data is only transmitted to TikTok's official API endpoints (open.tiktokapis.com) as required to fulfill the publishing function.

5. Data Retention

OAuth tokens are retained locally on your device until you explicitly delete the tiktok_token.json file or revoke access via your TikTok account settings. We do not maintain any server-side copy of your data.

6. Your Rights

You may revoke Knowledgeable4u's access to your TikTok account at any time by:

  • Going to your TikTok Settings → Security → Apps and Websites → and revoking access for Knowledgeable4u
  • Deleting the tiktok_token.json file from your local device

7. TikTok Platform Compliance

Knowledgeable4u uses the TikTok Content Posting API solely to publish educational video content. We comply with TikTok's Platform Terms, Developer Terms, and Community Guidelines. We do not use the API to engage in spam, inauthentic behavior, or any activity prohibited by TikTok's policies.

8. Children's Privacy

This application is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the application after changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: knowledgeable4u@gmail.com
